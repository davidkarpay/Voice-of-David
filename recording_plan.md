# Voice Cloning Recording Plan - Phase 2
## Target: 160 additional samples (4 hours total)

### Session Breakdown (8 sessions × ~30 minutes each)

**Session 1-2: Phonetically Balanced (40 samples each)**
- Harvard Sentences (phonetically complete)
- Record 20 samples per session, 2 sessions
- Focus: Coverage of all English phonemes

**Session 3-4: Conversational Speech (40 samples each)**
- Natural speaking, not reading
- Talk about your day, explain concepts, tell stories
- Focus: Natural prosody and rhythm

**Session 5-6: Varied Prosody (40 samples each)**
- Questions, exclamations, statements
- Different emotional tones
- Focus: Expressiveness range

**Session 7-8: Domain-Specific (40 samples)**
- Legal terminology and phrasing (your domain)
- Commands for voice assistant
- Focus: Task-specific vocabulary

### Recording Guidelines

**Quality standards:**
- Quiet environment (same as before)
- Consistent microphone position
- Natural speaking pace
- 8-30 seconds per sample
- No reading artifacts (sound natural!)

**File naming:**
- item_041.wav through item_200.wav
- Keep consistent format

**After each session:**
- Listen to recordings
- Delete and re-record any that sound off
- Add to metadata immediately while fresh
